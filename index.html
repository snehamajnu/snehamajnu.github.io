About Me

I'm a Data Analyst with a passion for transforming messy, real-world data into clear insights that drive decisions. I specialize in end-to-end analytics — from building ETL pipelines and SQL data models to crafting executive-level BI dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can actually act on.

Beyond analytics, I'm deeply drawn to Machine Learning and AI — particularly applied ML for forecasting, NLP-powered tools, and the growing space where LLMs meet business intelligence. I enjoy building systems that don't just report what happened, but predict what's next.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science (Data Analytics) from Rajagiri College of Social Sciences (2026) and am a SAP Hackfest 2025 Top 100 Nationwide Finalist for MindTrack — an LLM-powered mental wellness tracker.
